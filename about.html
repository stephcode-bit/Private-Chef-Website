My Story

About Me

A journey of passion, precision, and the pursuit of culinary excellence

Hello, I'm Alexander

Turning Fresh Ingredients into Memories

My love affair with food began in my grandmother's kitchen, where I first understood that cooking is more than following recipes—it's about creating moments of joy and connection. That early lesson has guided every dish I've created since.

After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America and spending over a decade in some of the country's most acclaimed restaurants, I discovered my true calling: bringing the restaurant experience into people's homes. There's something magical about cooking in a client's kitchen, understanding their preferences, and watching their faces light up with that first perfect bite.

Today, I specialize in creating personalized dining experiences that range from intimate dinners for two to grand celebrations for fifty. Every menu I create tells a story, and I'm honored to help write those stories for my clients.

My Approach

Culinary Philosophy

01

Ingredient-Driven

I let exceptional ingredients shine. From farm-fresh produce to sustainably sourced seafood, I build dishes around what's at its peak, ensuring every bite bursts with natural flavor.

02

Seasonally Inspired

Nature provides a roadmap for the best flavors. I embrace seasonal cooking not just because it tastes better, but because it connects my clients to the rhythm of the earth.

03

Personal Connection

Every client has a unique palate and story. I take time to understand your preferences, dietary needs, and the occasion to craft an experience that feels deeply personal.

04

Technical Excellence

Years of classical training underpin every dish. From perfect sears to delicate sauces, I bring professional technique to ensure restaurant-quality results in your home.

Training & Experience

Worked closely with Alice Waters' team, deepening my commitment to seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients and sustainable cooking practices.

Led the culinary team at this acclaimed Bay Area restaurant, earning recognition from the James Beard Foundation and local food publications.

Launched my private chef practice, bringing years of fine dining experience directly to clients' homes. Now serving discerning clients throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.
