Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas do you serve?

I primarily serve the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Francisco, Marin County, the Peninsula, and Silicon Valley. For events outside this area, travel arrangements can be made—just ask!

How far in advance should I book?

For private dining, I recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. For larger events and catering, 4-8 weeks is ideal. However, I occasionally have last-minute availability—don't hesitate to reach out!

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in accommodating all dietary needs including gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, vegetarian, keto, and food allergies. During our consultation, we'll discuss all requirements to ensure everyone enjoys a safe and delicious meal.

What kitchen equipment do you need?

A standard home kitchen with a working stove, oven, and basic cookware is typically sufficient. I bring specialized equipment, knives, and any additional tools needed. If you have concerns about your kitchen setup, let's discuss it during the consultation.

Do you provide wine and beverages?

I can provide wine pairing recommendations and assist with beverage selection. For an additional fee, I can also source wines and beverages for your event. Alternatively, I'm happy to work with whatever you choose to provide.
